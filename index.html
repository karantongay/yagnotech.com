What We Do

AI & Data Services

End-to-end consulting from strategy to production — no hand-holding required.

AI & ML Engineering

Design and deploy AI-driven pipelines, LLM integrations, recommender systems, and predictive analytics using Databricks, Airflow, and cloud-native ML platforms.

Data Privacy & Governance

Privacy-by-design architectures compliant with GDPR, CCPA, CPRA and HIPAA. Differential privacy, PII anonymization, DSAR automation, and data lineage — built in from day one.

Cloud Data Platform Build

Greenfield or migration — we architect modern data lakehouse and warehouse platforms on Azure (Databricks, Synapse, Fabric), AWS (Redshift, Glue, Athena) or GCP (BigQuery).

Cloud Migration

On-prem to cloud, cross-cloud or SaaS to cloud-native. We've migrated Hadoop, Snowflake, QlikView and legacy warehouses to modern Azure and AWS stacks with zero data loss.

Data Engineering & ETL

Scalable, observable ETL/ELT pipelines using Azure Data Factory, Apache Spark, Airflow, AWS Glue, Talend and Informatica — built for reliability and governed from the start.

Technical Consulting & Architecture

Fractional data architect and CTO support. Architecture reviews, stack selection, DevOps setup, and cross-functional alignment between engineering, legal and compliance teams.

About Us

Deep Expertise. Real Delivery.

YagnoTech Inc. is a Canadian technology consulting firm specializing in AI engineering, enterprise data platforms and data privacy. We've built and shipped data infrastructure at organizations like Microsoft, Vancouver Airport Authority, ICBC, and Beatport.

Our principal, Karan Tongay, holds an MSc in Computer Science (Data Privacy) from the University of Victoria, is a published IEEE researcher, and carries AWS and Microsoft certifications. His work spans differential privacy, LLM integration, cloud migrations and large-scale ETL — across Azure, AWS and GCP.

We engage as embedded consultants, bringing senior-level craft and accountability to every engagement — whether that's a six-week migration or a multi-year data platform build.
